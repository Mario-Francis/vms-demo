Proposal Overview

This proposal outlines the scope, timeline, and costs associated with providing backup engineering solutions for critical infrastructure, ensuring uninterrupted power during outages.

Scope of Work

  • Site Survey: A detailed evaluation of the site to determine the appropriate backup power system requirements.
  • Supply and Installation: Procurement and installation of backup generators and associated electrical infrastructure.
  • Testing and Commissioning: Full testing of the backup system to ensure its functionality and safety.
  • Ongoing Maintenance: Regular maintenance, including inspections, fuel checks, and system testing to ensure optimal performance.

Timeline

The project is expected to take approximately 6 weeks from commencement, with the following key milestones:

  • Site Survey: 1 Week
  • Installation: 3 Weeks
  • Testing and Commissioning: 2 Weeks

Pricing

The total estimated cost for the backup engineering solution is $50,000, including equipment, installation, and 1 year of maintenance. A detailed breakdown is provided below:

  • Backup Generator: $30,000
  • Installation: $12,000
  • Testing and Commissioning: $5,000
  • Maintenance (1 year): $3,000
