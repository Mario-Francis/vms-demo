Overview:

This Request for Proposal (RFP) seeks bids from qualified vendors for the supply, installation, and ongoing maintenance of backup generators. The selected vendor will be responsible for ensuring that the backup generator system is capable of maintaining operations during power outages, providing an uninterrupted power supply to critical facilities and equipment.

Scope of Work: The vendor will provide a comprehensive solution for a reliable backup power system, which includes:

  • Supply of Backup Generators: Provide the appropriate size and model of backup generators based on power requirements (specific to the buildingâ€™s size, critical equipment, and estimated energy consumption). Ensure that the generators are fuel-efficient and capable of running continuously for extended periods.
  • Installation: Full installation of the backup generator system, including electrical connections, fuel lines, and any required infrastructure. Conduct necessary site surveys to determine the optimal location for installation.
  • Testing and Commissioning: Perform thorough testing of the backup system to ensure it operates correctly under various load conditions. Commission the system with the appropriate safety measures in place to guarantee the system's performance.
  • Ongoing Maintenance and Support: Provide a maintenance plan for the backup generator system, including regular checks, oil changes, fuel system inspections, and battery maintenance. Offer a 24/7 support and emergency response service in case of any malfunction or failure.
  • Training: Provide training for the on-site team on how to operate, maintain, and troubleshoot the backup generator.

Requirements:

  • Vendors must demonstrate proven experience in the installation, commissioning, and maintenance of backup generators.
  • The proposed solution must meet all safety standards, local regulations, and environmental guidelines.
  • The vendor must provide warranty and post-installation support for at least 3 years.
  • The solution must be scalable to accommodate future expansion of power needs.

Proposal Submission Instructions:

  • All proposals must be submitted by specified deadline.
  • Proposals should include a detailed breakdown of the generator models, installation process, and maintenance schedule.
  • The vendor should also provide references from previous clients for whom similar services were performed.

Evaluation Criteria: Proposals will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Compliance with technical specifications and scope of work.
  • Cost-effectiveness and value for money.
  • Vendor experience and reputation in the industry.
  • Warranty, support, and maintenance options.

For any clarifications or questions, please contact us.
